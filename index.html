About T.E.T.R.I.S.

T.E.T.R.I.S. began development in 2007 as a joint project between the Division of Cancer Medicine IT team and the Ambulatory Treatment Center team.

The aim was, and still is today, to provide a tool that will help to improve the quality of care that our patients receive by focusing on the efficient use of resources.

To date, the use of T.E.T.R.I.S. has expanded beyond its roots within ATC as the real-time data that it provides has proven valuable to teams in other departments and centers throughout the institution.
